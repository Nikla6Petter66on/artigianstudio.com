Studios

Chief Engineer

After I graduated from SAE Institute of NY in 2008 with GPA 4/4, I took my first steps at StudioBeat2, assisting a multi platinum award winning engineer, Maurizio “Irko” Sera (Kanye West, Jay-Z…). After one year I became manager and chief engineer at the same studio, which was one control room in a bigger structure called Magister Recording Area, owned by another famous engineer, Andrea Valfrè (Umberto Tozzi, Luna Pop…). Having worked next to engineers like Irko and Andrea, has helped me to aim high and improve my skills faster.

Today

Learnings

In 2014, after gaining experience, I began to work also in the writing and production phase. Firstly at the side of a great producer like Nicola Zanetti (Dj Fango) as arranger, subsequently alone. In 2015 I have founded Artigian Studio, sharing another amazing analog control room inside the same structure: The Fishbowl Studio. In 2018 I moved to Berlin and nowadays I mainly offer production and mixing services, although I still work as external engineer at Magister Recording Area.
